Structure Funding

Fund Based / Non Fund Based

To Customers

Structured Financing

We provide our special services to the eligible clients for their spontaneous and unplanned financial requirements for their complexed financial needs. We assist our clients in finding the right and workable financial solutions for their needs.

Asset life cycle Management

In the asset base finance business, asset is the primary security, thus we focus on the entire life cycle of the asset. We provide our expert management services for the asset life cycle right from the procurement of the asset to its deployment, manoeuvring, maintenance and disposal.

Working Capital Financial assistance

Working capital is a very critical component in the business. We cater the short term and long terms working capital requirements of our clients on the easy and viable terms. We assist our client in assessing the present and future working capital requirements, help in making the proposal and place them to the right financial institution for the easy and quick disposal.

Loan Syndication

We offer our prime services in loan syndication for catering the bigger requirement of the large size customers. We have an extensive client networking in the market and we cater various kind of the financial requirement of our customers. We are also in the process of developing our advantageous networking with the various banks and financial institutions through which we can channelize the requirement of either side for the ease of the business.

Project Advisory Services

We provides consultancy services in planning of a project or program, develop effective governance structures, and assess project and process performance focused on better conceptualizing and planning of capital investments. Our services include the practical cash flow analysis factual viability analysis which is the critical key factors of a project. Our Projects Advisory helps enhance performance of your capital investments and projects. The experienced team enables project stakeholders achieve success in their projects, by integrating industry experience with hands-on expertise, technical and financial competencies, effective methods and time-tested digital and technology tools.

Leasing solution

Through our leasing solution services, you can own equipment without purchasing it. Moreover if you are looking to increase your liquidity and manage cash flow better, our assistance in leasing the equipment will help you to upgrade your business with the acquisition of the required equipment in your fleet, without disrupting your working capital and business credit line. This can maximizing your profits, transfer the ownership risk, and yet enjoy all benefits of ownership through the customised leasing structure most suitable for your business requirements.

Services for existing equipment

Our expert technical team is also able to cater your needs for the fleet of your existing construction equipment. We provide services for servicing, repairing, transportation and refurbishing of existing equipment as per your requirement. We also provide assistance in getting refinance on existing equipment. Our team also can help you in selling your existing equipment at good price.

Project operation and maintenance

We also provide assistance in running project and maintenance in various ways. Our expert team is available to evaluate the requirements of operation assistance and maintenance requirements in a running project.

To Bank/Financial Institutions

Critical Account Recovery and Resolution plan
